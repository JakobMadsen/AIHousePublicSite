AI in Daily Operations

Target group: Administrative staff | Duration: 1 day

Purpose

This workshop gives administrative employees practical experience using AI as an everyday tool. The goal is to build confidence in how AI can streamline their work  from document handling and communication to report drafts, proposals and marketing. The company also gains an overview of the biggest opportunities and recommended next steps.

Content

  • AI as daily helper: How AI supports document work, emails, report writing, proposals and marketing tasks.
  • Hands-on training: Participants work with their own tasks and documents so learning is directly applicable.
  • Understand limitations: What AI can  and cannot  so employees use it realistically and safely.
  • Dialogue and sparring: Together we identify where AI can free time and create the most value in daily operations.

Process

  1. Preparation: Short alignment with the company about typical administrative tasks and relevant examples.
  2. Workshop: 1 day mix of presentations, exercises and discussion  focused on participants own cases.
  3. Follow-up: The company receives a report with improvement suggestions, top use cases and recommended next steps.

Outcomes

  • Employees who confidently use AI for daily tasks.
  • Faster workflows  tasks that took hours can take minutes.
  • A shared understanding of where AI creates value.
  • A concrete list of opportunities plus a suggested roadmap.
