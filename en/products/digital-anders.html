Preserve Knowledge  "Digital Anders"

Target group: Companies with key staff near retirement | Duration: 24 week project

Background

Many organisations depend on employees with decades of experience. When they leave, critical knowledge disappearsoften undocumented.

Purpose

Digital Anders converts tacit knowledge into a searchable, usable digital resource. The AI acts like a colleague who always remembers and shares insight.

Content

  • Knowledge capture: Structured review of mails, docs, historic cases.
  • Interviews: Surfacing unwritten expertise.
  • AI model: Trained on curated material; answers contextually.

Example: Customer asks: Why does product A fail in heavy rain?

Digital Anders: Often due to missing the weather-sealed variant. Use version B.

Process

  1. Scoping: Map domains & key holders.
  2. Capture: Interviews + document ingestion.
  3. AI training: Adaptation and tuning.
  4. Deployment: Released internally as knowledge base.

Outcomes

  • Preserved critical expertise.
  • Faster onboarding.
  • Reduced single-person dependency.
  • A digital colleague that retains context.
