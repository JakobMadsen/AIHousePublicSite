AI Intro Workshop

Target group: Leadership & key employees | Duration: 3 hours

Purpose

The AI Intro Workshop is the first step for companies wanting to understand how artificial intelligence can create real value. Instead of generic trends and buzzwords we focus on your business, daily realities and challenges.

Content

  • Introduction to AI: What AI is, what it can do and where the limits are.
  • Concrete examples: We review cases from similar SMEs  successes and pitfalls.
  • Interactive session: Participants try tools like ChatGPT, Copilot and image analysis hands-on.
  • Low-hanging fruits: Joint identification of immediate value areas.

Process

  1. Preparation: Short meeting to understand industry, processes and challenges.
  2. Workshop: 3 hours of presentation, examples, interactive exercises and discussion.
  3. Follow-up: Short report with key insights, next step ideas and low-hanging fruits list.

Outcomes

  • Realistic understanding of AI: hype vs. usable.
  • Overview of concrete opportunities.
  • Identified quick wins implementable fast.
  • A shared internal language about AI and strategic use.
