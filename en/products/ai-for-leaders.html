AI for Leaders  from uncertainty to leverage

Target group: Leaders and middle management | Duration: 1 day (extendable)

Purpose

Many leaders feel AI is a train leaving without them. This workshop helps you regain clarity and see how AI can become a support in your role  without needing to be a technology expert. Focus: free time, strengthen decision quality, and allow you to concentrate on people and strategy.

Content

  • AI in leadership routine: Planning, meeting prep, reporting and decision material.
  • From uncertainty to strength: Practical exercises that build confidence treating AI as a tool not a threat.
  • Leading with AI: Use AI to focus on core tasks  people, strategy and business.
  • Personal application: Participants work with mails, agendas or leadership reports.

Process

  1. Preparation: Short discussion about leadership tasks & challenges.
  2. Workshop: 1 day of talks, cases and hands-on practice.
  3. Follow-up: Personal plan describing where AI best augments the role.

Outcomes

  • A clear understanding of how AI strengthens leadership.
  • Confidence using AI pragmatically.
  • Concrete methods to free time and raise decision quality.
  • Personalized list of AI leverage points.
